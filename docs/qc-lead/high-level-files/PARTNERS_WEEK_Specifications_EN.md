PARTNERS WEEK® is an international event and ecosystem dedicated to celebrating and  recognizing business partnerships using blockchain technology. 

Points will serve as 'pre-tokens' convertible 1:1 to utility tokens once tokenomics are  established.

**✓ CONFIRMED ELEMENTS** 

**Payment System** 

CHF only via Stripe (no cryptocurrency payments) 

**Custodial Wallets** 

Automatically created for companies A & B via Tatum API 

**Purchase Flow** 

Company A buys distinction, pays in CHF, receives NFT \+ 10% points. Company B receives NFT \+ 10% points. 10% to PARTNERS WEEK pool (30% total). 

**NFT Distinctions** 

3 types: Philanthrope, Investisseur, Pont \- non-sellable, transferable 1x to non-custodial  wallet 

**NFT Metadata** 

Company A (buyer), Company B (recipient), distinction type, year, country, buyer's class **Smart Contract**   
Linked contracts for A & B. Prevents same distinction between A & B same year (anti-return) **Individual Class**   
Can only buy 'Pont' at CHF 19 (no points distribution) 

**Public Badge & Directory** 

Official badge 'Issued by PARTNERS WEEK®' linking to partnersweek.com. Public directory with search by company/distinctions/Awards (IPFS) 

**E-commerce Catalog** 

3 annual distinctions, modifiable by admin
